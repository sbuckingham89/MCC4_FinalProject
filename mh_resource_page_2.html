Free or Low-Cost



Student Mental Health Services

You have options for accessing mental health services:

On-Campus Resources

Visit Campus Counseling Center or Health Center in the Health Building.

Schedule a time online.

Free student-to-student counseling or session with trained supportive listener.

Find a time to chat.

Attend a free student support group (virtual or in-person).

See the Campus Mental Health Calendar for meeting schedule.

Apply for Mental Wellness Journey sponsorship and get funds to pay for licensed therapy sessions, session-time childcare, transport or other support.

Use our campus library to find articles, self-help books and insightful reading material.

Attend free wellness courses, exercise/health classes or other wellness events.

See the Campus Mental Health Calendar for events.

Off-Campus Resources

Find a licensed therapist or counselor offering pro-bono or income-based student rates, or schedule with a local training clinic or hospital.

Visit a free off-campus support group.

Schedule an income-based counseling session at the local YMCA.
